ANIMATION

Animation is the process of making the illusion of motion and the illusion of changeby means of the rapid display of a sequence of images that minimally differ from each other.

While there were several predecessors, the 17th century invention of the magic lantern provided the first apparatus with which convincing moving images have been created.
